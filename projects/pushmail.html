A live browser-based push and contact service

Push-Mail is a closed-source service that I use for browser notifications and lightweight private contact flows. The public site is live and usable, but I am deliberately keeping the technical internals out of the public write-up for now.

Closed source Live service Desktop + mobile friendly
Open at push.port0.org Contact

What it does

The service is built around a simple goal: make it easy to receive private messages and push notifications through the browser without turning the experience into a complicated full messaging platform.

  • Provides a live browser-accessible push and messaging workflow.
  • Designed to stay lightweight and usable across desktop and mobile devices.
  • Works well as a practical contact and notification endpoint rather than just a demo page.
